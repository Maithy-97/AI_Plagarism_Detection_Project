About VerifiDoc

VerifiDoc is an AI-powered plagiarism detection tool that helps students, educators, and professionals ensure originality in their work.

Our system compares submitted content with multiple sources to detect similarities and provides instant results.It is designed to be fast, reliable, and accessible, with a simple user-friendly interface that makes checking plagiarism effortless.

Our mission is to promote academic integrity and authentic writing. We aim to be a trusted partner for originality verification, supporting the creation of unique and credible content.
